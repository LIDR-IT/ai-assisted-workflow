## Detailed Stakeholder Analysis

### 🔴 MANAGE CLOSELY (Critical Success Factors)

#### 1. Banking Regulators

**Organizations**: ECB, Banco de España, BaFin, ACPR, Bank of Italy

- **Power**: Extreme (Can block market access)
- **Interest**: High (PSD2 SCA compliance mandatory)
- **Influence**: Direct regulatory approval required
- **Concerns**:
  - Strong Customer Authentication technical standards
  - Operational resilience requirements
  - Consumer protection measures
- **Engagement Strategy**:
  - Quarterly compliance reports
  - Pre-submission technical consultations
  - Industry working group participation
- **Key Messages**: "Exceeds PSD2 RTS requirements, enhances consumer security"

#### 2. Data Protection Authorities (DPAs)

**Organizations**: CNIL (FR), ICO (UK), AEPD (ES), BfDI (DE)

- **Power**: Extreme (€20M fine authority)
- **Interest**: High (Article 9 special category data)
- **Influence**: GDPR compliance certification required
- **Concerns**:
  - Explicit consent mechanisms for domain-specific processing
  - Data minimization and purpose limitation
  - Cross-border data transfer safeguards
  - domain-specific template irreversibility
- **Engagement Strategy**:
  - DPIA consultations with lead supervisory authority
  - Monthly compliance status reports
  - Joint privacy impact assessments
- **Key Messages**: "Privacy by Design, explicit consent, minimal data processing"

#### 3. Tier-1 Banking Clients

**Organizations**: BBVA, Santander, BNP Paribas, Deutsche Bank

- **Power**: Very High (Revenue impact)
- **Interest**: Very High (Competitive advantage)
- **Influence**: Product roadmap and feature prioritization
- **Concerns**:
  - False rejection rates impacting customer experience
  - Integration complexity with existing systems
  - Total cost of ownership vs security benefits
  - Regulatory compliance attestations
- **Engagement Strategy**:
  - Monthly executive steering committees
  - Dedicated technical account managers
  - Joint go-to-market initiatives
- **Key Messages**: "Seamless UX, proven ROI, regulatory confidence"

#### 4. {{CLIENT_NAME}} CISO & Legal Counsel

**Internal Stakeholders**: Chief Information Security Officer, General Counsel

- **Power**: Very High (Can halt development)
- **Interest**: Very High (Legal and security liability)
- **Influence**: Security architecture and legal framework approval
- **Concerns**:
  - domain-specific template encryption standards
  - Incident response procedures
  - Vendor risk management for cloud services
  - International data transfer mechanisms
- **Engagement Strategy**:
  - Weekly security review meetings
  - Continuous security testing and monitoring
  - Legal framework validation checkpoints
- **Key Messages**: "Security-first design, compliance by default, risk mitigation"

### 🟡 KEEP SATISFIED (Important Influencers)

#### 5. European Banking Authority (EBA)

**Role**: EU banking regulation coordination

- **Power**: High (Regulatory Technical Standards)
- **Interest**: Medium (Industry-wide standards)
- **Influence**: PSD2 interpretation and guidance
- **Engagement Strategy**:
  - Submit responses to consultation papers
  - Participate in industry roundtables
  - Monitor guidance updates quarterly

#### 6. ISO 27001 Certification Body

**Organizations**: BSI, AENOR, TÜV

- **Power**: High (Certification authority)
- **Interest**: Medium (Certification compliance)
- **Influence**: Security management system validation
- **Engagement Strategy**:
  - Annual surveillance audits
  - Proactive gap assessments
  - Documentation alignment reviews

### 🟢 KEEP INFORMED (Operational Success)

#### 7. Banking IT Security Teams

**Role**: Technical implementation and integration

- **Power**: Medium (Technical implementation)
- **Interest**: High (System security and performance)
- **Influence**: Integration success and user adoption
- **Concerns**:
  - API security and rate limiting
  - System availability and disaster recovery
  - Integration testing and certification
- **Engagement Strategy**:
  - Technical webinars and documentation
  - Developer sandbox environments
  - Direct technical support channels
- **Key Messages**: "Robust APIs, comprehensive documentation, proven scalability"

#### 8. Fraud Prevention Analysts

**Role**: Anti-fraud system integration

- **Power**: Medium (Operational influence)
- **Interest**: High (Fraud detection effectiveness)
- **Influence**: Algorithm tuning and false positive optimization
- **Concerns**:
  - domain-specific spoofing detection accuracy
  - Integration with existing fraud scoring
  - Real-time performance under load
- **Engagement Strategy**:
  - Monthly performance reviews
  - Joint fraud pattern analysis
  - Algorithm optimization workshops

### 🔵 MONITOR (Broader Ecosystem)

#### 9. General Banking Customers

**Role**: End users of domain-specific authentication

- **Power**: Low (Individual influence)
- **Interest**: Low (Indirect interaction)
- **Influence**: Long-term adoption and satisfaction
- **Concerns**:
  - Privacy of domain-specific data
  - Ease of use and accessibility
  - Alternative authentication methods
- **Engagement Strategy**:
  - User experience research and testing
  - Privacy communication campaigns
  - Customer support optimization

### Engagement Triggers

#### Regulatory Changes

- **Trigger**: New PSD2 guidance or GDPR interpretation
- **Action**: Immediate impact assessment and stakeholder notification
- **Timeline**: 48-hour initial response, 2-week full analysis

#### Security Incidents

- **Trigger**: Any security incident affecting domain-specific data
- **Action**: Stakeholder notification per incident response plan
- **Timeline**: 72-hour breach notification requirement

#### Technical Milestones

- **Trigger**: Major release or architecture changes
- **Action**: Technical briefings for relevant stakeholders
- **Timeline**: 2-week advance notice minimum

## Contingency Plans

### Stakeholder Opposition Management

#### Scenario: Regulatory Objection

**Trigger**: Regulator raises compliance concerns
**Response Plan**:

1. Immediate technical consultation within 48h
2. Legal counsel engagement and compliance review
3. Alternative technical approach development
4. Stakeholder communication and expectation management

#### Scenario: Client Technical Issues

**Trigger**: Major client reports integration problems
**Response Plan**:

1. Emergency technical support activation
2. Senior engineering escalation within 4h
3. Alternative solution development if needed
4. Relationship recovery and confidence rebuilding

#### Scenario: Privacy Authority Investigation

**Trigger**: DPA initiates formal investigation
**Response Plan**:

1. Legal response team activation
2. Full documentation and audit trail preparation
3. Technical expert testimony preparation
4. Public relations and client communication strategy
